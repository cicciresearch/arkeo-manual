    ARKEO Manual¶

    Welcome¶

    The ARKEO platform is a modular multi-functional characterization system designed for advanced optoelectronic and photovoltaic device testing.

    This manual provides a complete overview of:

    • System hardware and accessories
    • Software architecture and routines
    • Scientific background of measurement methods
    • Step-by-step operational guidance
